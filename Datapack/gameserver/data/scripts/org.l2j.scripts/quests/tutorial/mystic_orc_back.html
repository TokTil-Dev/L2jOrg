Newbie Helper:
Did you forget you have a mission? Go retrieve the Blue Gemstones.You must defeat the Gremlins in order to get the Blue Gemstones.
Like I told you earlier, move the cursor to the Gremlin and left-click. Then the cursor will switch to a sword. Click the left mouse button again to attack.
Use Soul Cry magic before attacking. This will improve your chances greatly. To activate Soul Cry magic, press the F2 key, and again to de-activate it.

Once you finish the mission, I'll give you a useful item. Good luck.
